3. **All five videos are available and public** — None are private, deleted, or region-blocked.

4. **"Seven Levels" terminology** — The deck uses "Seven Levels" in connection with Cole Medin, but this terminology comes from Chase AI's video. Cole Medin's own related content uses "6 Levels" not "7 Levels".
